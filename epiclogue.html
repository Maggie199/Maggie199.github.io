down on their luck

There is a backstory to everyone's struggle, and our idea is for users to piece together those stories and identify ways to bring attention and opportunity to people down on their luck.

social impact, crowdsourced

We view each story as an EpicLogue -- a narrative of how everyday users can help someone get back on their feet -- that utilizes the Internet to make social impact more accessible.
