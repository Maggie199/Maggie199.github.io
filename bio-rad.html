Success Business

Bio-Rad Laboratories(NYSE: BIO and BIOb) ranks among the top five life science companies in the world and maintains a solid reputation for quality and innovation.

Growing products portfolio

As Bio-Rad gets more products in its portfolio, it seeks to sell more commoditized technologies via web, so that sales representatives could focus on higher value products.

10+ year old website

The bio-rad.com was launched back in 1997, and we saw lots of room for improvement to make it a trendy website: hero images, card layouts, responsive design, etc...

Information Overflow

As the information architecture kept growing in the past 10+ years, it takes 6+ clicks to navigate to a single product's page, where all contents are displayed on the same level.

Contextual inquiry

Conducted interviews on a wide range of internal stakeholders, including product managers, sales representatives, technical support and customer service, to learn about the most typical scenario when customers decide they need a thermal cycler, what information are needed by them to make a purchase decision, how do they interact with bio-rad to get those info, and most importatnly what kind of group could be our target users of online purchasing.

Researched on the website of 6 top competitors in life science field and 6 non-peer vendors to compare their features and content, as well as branstorm the best online purchasing experience.

  • 10 interviews on internal stakeholders
  • Competitive usability evaluations on 12 peer and non-peer websites
