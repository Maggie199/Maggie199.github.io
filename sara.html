Motor vehicle accidents claim unnecessary lives. When all victims are unconscious, it takes time for bystanders to find them and call emergency services.

information severity and accuracy

After accidents occur, it's hard for shocked victims to report necessary info in the shortest time, while bystanders cannot provide relevant medical details.

Reduce Vehicular Fatalities via Automated Signal

1

Ideation

Conducted research on reported car accidents over the past two decades and ways to reduce the number of vehicular fatalities. Also evaluated the positioning of SARA in comparison to other emergency systems.

Designed the workflow of the system and identified design objectives: a) voice message with exact text that quantifies a legitimate 911 phone call and b) a “cancel” button to terminate SARA’s automated response and reduce the false positives that inherent with any automated response system.

  • Flow Chart
2

Initial design

Came up with an initial design for the cancel button based on features of already existent emergency services and our experiences of driving a motor vehicle.

  • Specifications of initial design
3

Paper prototypes

Created several paper prototypes that represent core screens and interactions from the information architecture diagram defined in step2.

4

Wireframes

Implemented wireframes designed in step3 with Keynote and improved it via group discussion.

5

Visual Target

Created visual targets to represent the wireframes in step4 at a shipping level of production quality, and deliver on the appropriate level of information and interaction clarity.
