Lack of UX resources

Here at Yelp, we don't have a formal UX team. Product managers and designers' intention to conduct UX tests are often hold back due to the cost of time and money.

Internal Staff As Audience

Most of employees are loyal users of our product, and have no involvement in the product design phase. They want their voice heard, and we want those voice.

Let Yelp employees help define our products
2015 Yelp internal hackathon 18

1

Ideation

Discussed with representatives of product managers and designers to learn about their painpoints and needs. Brainstormed the functionality of our app and prioritized them by group discussion and critiquing.

  • List of app functionalities on each page
2

Prototyping

Created prototypes to realize the functionalities designed in step1 with Adobe Photoshop and iteratively improved them via concept validation with key stakeholders.

  • Design Specifications
3

Implementation

Implemented the mockup designed in step2 using HTML, CSS, Javascript and python with Bootstrap framework. The final work is live at http://yelpux.appspot.com/, note that a @yelp.com email is required for log-in since it's internal only :)
